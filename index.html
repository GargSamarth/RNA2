About

After over 30 years of manufacturing and trading, RMA Milk food and packaging pvt ltd is still running with the committed, special touch that continues to make us dairy supplier of choice for many of India's top restaurants, hotels, bakeries, caterers and cafes. With our dedicated supply base and unparalleled service, we are able to ensure our customers receive a secure supply of dairy ingredients 365 days a year. We instigated as a small dairy manufacturing firm but, through organic growth and strategic acquisitions, RMA Milk food and packaging pvt ltd is now a major dairy ingredients manufacturer by expanding into Indore and North India. All milk procured by RMA Milk food and packaging pvt ltd is Farm Assured and adheres to the strictest hygiene standards. We are committed to supply our ghee with all the standards and hygiene to our faithful customers.
